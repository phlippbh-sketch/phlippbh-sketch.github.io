Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and handles your information when you connect your WHOOP account. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data We Access

By connecting your WHOOP account via OAuth, we may access the following data depending on the permissions you grant:

  • Recovery scores and HRV data
  • Sleep data and sleep performance
  • Workout and strain data
  • Body measurements (height, weight, max heart rate)
  • Daily physiological cycle data
  • Basic profile information

We only request the minimum data necessary to provide the app's functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your WHOOP data is used solely to provide and improve the features of this application. Specifically:

  • To display your health and fitness metrics within the app
  • To generate insights or summaries based on your data
  • To personalize your experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Access tokens from WHOOP are stored securely and are only used to make authorized requests on your behalf. We do not store raw WHOOP data longer than necessary to provide the service.

Third-Party Services

This app integrates with the WHOOP Developer API. Your use of WHOOP's services is also governed by WHOOP's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may revoke this app's access to your WHOOP data at any time through your WHOOP account settings. Upon revocation, we will no longer be able to access your data, and you may request deletion of any stored data by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
